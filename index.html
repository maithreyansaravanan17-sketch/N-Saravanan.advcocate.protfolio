About

Experienced Legal Advocate in Thiruvannamalai

Advocate N. Saravanan (B.A.,B.L.,) is a seasoned legal professional based in Polur, Thiruvannamalai district, Tamil Nadu. With 25+ years of dedicated practice, he brings deep knowledge of Indian law and an unwavering commitment to his clients.

He provides comprehensive legal services, ensuring every client receives honest counsel and strong representation — from initial consultation all the way through to resolution.

Conveniently located in Polur, he serves individuals, families, and businesses across Thiruvannamalai district and surrounding areas.
